I'm Nick.

Before that, I covered the video game industry, publishing everything from late-breaking stories about Valve Software & its Steam product to in-depth analyses on professional esports teams.

While I no longer call that my career, I've decided to call it a hobby.

While I still play games, I'm even more fascinated by how they work, why developers make the decisions that they do, and how fans can become a more constructive force for the games they love (instead of a decidedly destructive one).

Come read my blog! Or don't, I'm writing it for me anyway.
